FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

IS MY MONEY SAFE?▼
Yes. Your capital is backed by insured luxury inventory. We recommend starting with $50 to verify payouts before investing larger amounts. All goods are insured by Lloyd's of London, and transactions are recorded on-chain.
HOW DO YOU GENERATE RETURNS?▼
We pool investor capital to purchase rare luxury goods — watches, designer clothing, sneakers, jewelry — at 30-50% below retail through platform partnerships and private dealers. Items are resold on Grailed, eBay, Chrono24, Vestiaire Collective, and StockX at full market price.
HOW DO WITHDRAWALS WORK?▼
Request a withdrawal anytime from your dashboard. Funds are processed within 15 minutes to 2 hours. No penalties, no lock-up periods. You can withdraw earned profits or your full balance.
WHAT CURRENCIES DO YOU ACCEPT?▼
We accept USDT (TRC20), ETH, BTC, SOL, BNB, and credit/debit card payments. Withdrawals are processed in the same currency you deposited.
IS THERE A REFERRAL PROGRAM?▼
Yes! Earn 3% of each referral's first investment. Share your unique code from the dashboard. No limit on referrals.
CAN I HAVE MULTIPLE PLANS?▼
Absolutely. You can invest in Silver, Gold, and Platinum simultaneously. Each position is tracked separately with its own payout schedule.
